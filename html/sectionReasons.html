Reasons to visit Ukraine’s capital:

Rich history and culture

The city is more than 1500 old. It remembers great medieval past, ups and downs of empires, world wars, soviet regime, revolutions. So just imagine how many great stories the city keeps

Unique atmosphere

Kyiv has an atmosphere that you can’t find anywhere in Europe. It’s the mixture of ancient Slavic and European cultures, Soviet brutalism and Ukrainian spirit. Kyiv is rebellious and conservative, luxurious and shabby at the same time

Beautiful views

1/2 of Kyiv’s territory is occupied by parks and gardens. You can’t walk far without passing one. Picturesque Dnipro river, which runs through the middle of the city, provides sandy beaches and recreation areas in the very heart of the capital

Reasonable prices

Dinner bill = 8$
Ticket to museum = 2$
Subway ticket = 0,2$
Cup of coffee = 1$
Taxi from airport = 7$
Cocktail in bar = 3$

Entertainment

Kyiv’s got almost everything you need.
Traditional food markets
More than 150 museums
Stunning parks and river beaches
Numerous night clubs
Cultural events and festivals
Various art spaces
Restaurants and street food spots

Good location

Kyiv is the very heart of Europe. If you live in Rome or Tel Aviv the flight will be approximately 3 hours. So for some of you it can be the weekend tour.
